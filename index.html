About me

A few months ago I decided to change professions. Earlier, I worked in a corporation as an accountant specialist, but at some point it stopped being challenging. I considered a couple of options - changing my role inside of the company, changing companies, or changing my profession. For quite a while I explored the first two to finally decide on the third one. When I do something, I do it for 100%, so I quit my job and started my journey into programming world.

Why programming?

I learned some C++ when I was at school. Even though I don't remember the language, I know that I enjoyed it a lot, so i figured that it might be a good idea to start over with something that I know I like. Ok, but C++ doesn't have that much in common with HTML, CSS nor JavaScript, you say! And you're right. I started my self-development from checking out some different paths to finally decide on Front-End. It is very satisfying when, on one hand, in just a few lines of code I can change a website completely and, on the other hand, there is much more to learn and do then just write a few simple lines.
